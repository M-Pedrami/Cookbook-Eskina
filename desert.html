Chocolate Cake

EASY
Taste the richness

Indulge in the ultimate chocolatey delight with this simple yet decadent chocolate cake recipe.

Ingredients: 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour. 1 and 3/4 cups granulated sugar. 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder. 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder. 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda. 1 tsp salt. 2 eggs. 1 cup buttermilk. 1/2 cup vegetable oil. 2 tsp pure vanilla extract. 1 cup boiling water. Duration: 35 mins

Walnut Cake

MEDIUM
Walnut creamy wonder

Wether you want a more pronounced walnut flavor or a milder one. Enjoy your walnut cake!

Ingredients: 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar. 4 large eggs. 2 cups all-purpose flour. 1 teaspoon baking powder. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda. 1/2 teaspoon salt. 1 cup buttermilk. 113 grams of unsalted butter. 200 grams of granulated sugar. 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract. 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts. Duration: 30 mins

Cream Cake

EASY
Tasty wellness

These ingredients should be enough to make a delicious cream cake. Adjust the flavor and decorations according to your preferences.

Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour. 2 teaspoons baking powder. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda. 1/2 teaspoon salt. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened. 1 cup granulated sugar. 2 large eggs. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. 1 cup buttermilk. For the Cream Filling: 2 cups heavy cream. Duration: 45 mins

Strawberry Creamy Cake

EASY
Melt in your mouth

For the "Melt in your mouth recipe", follow the steps below for a delicious strawberry cream cake:

Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour. 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened. 1/2 cup vegetable oil. 4 large eggs. 1 cup whole milk. 2 teaspoons baking powder. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. 1/2 teaspoon salt. For the Strawberry Filling: 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced. Duration: 35 mins

Fruity Delight

Natures piquant

Fruity Delight is made with love and also with this simple yet decadent fruit dessert recipe you can create a magical feeling.

Ingredients: Fresh Fruits (Choose a variety based on your preferences or what's in season): Strawberries. Blueberries. Raspberries. Blackberries. Sliced bananas. Sliced kiwi. Sliced peaches. Sliced mangoes. Grapes. Cherries (pitted). Sweeteners: Granulated sugar. Honey. Maple syrup. Brown sugar. Flavor Enhancers. Duration: 30 mins

Dessert Eskina

Our specialty

Created by our in-house chef allows for creativity and the use of premium ingredients for our special "Dessert EsKiNA".

Choco Awesome

EASY
Complient of Eskina

The chocolate awesome is our in-house recipe. Quick and simple for making a delicious chocolate dessert.

Ingredients: 4 ounces (113 grams) of high-quality dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher), chopped. 113 grams of unsalted butter. 200 grams of granulated sugar. 2 eggs. 2 egg yolks. 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract. 1/4 cup (30 grams) of all-purpose flour. Powdered sugar for dusting. Fresh berries Whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. Duration: 20 mins
